# External Images of / about S.T.A.N.D Mentorship — Catalog & Rights Notes

**Org:** S.T.A.N.D Mentorship — legal name *Stepping Towards A New Destiny Corporation* (EIN 82-1192567), a Maryland 501(c)(3) youth-mentorship nonprofit for young men of color in Baltimore City/County + Howard County. Founder/ED: **Tigana S. ("Coach") Duncan**.
**Scope:** Publicly available images that exist **beyond** the org's own site (standmentorship.org). This is a **reference catalog of URLs + descriptions** — *nothing here has been downloaded.* Each row has an honest rights/usage note.
**Compiled:** 2026-06-19 · derrick@digitaldog.io

> **Disambiguation done:** All items below are verified to the Maryland / Tigana-Duncan / Howard County org. Excluded: unrelated "STAND" orgs, "Project STAND" (Memphis), "United We Stand" CACs/programs in other states, and FOX45's "Happening This Weekend: Youth Mentorship Event" (that segment is **Couture Mentoring Inc.**, *not* S.T.A.N.D).

> **Rights legend**
> - **GOV** = Howard County Government press/photo — typically reusable **with credit** ("Howard County Government"), but MD county works are *not* automatically public domain; confirm with the County Office of Public Information before publishing.
> - **PARTNER** = posted by an org partner (Columbia Community Care = umbrella/host; Community Foundation of Howard County; Wilde Lake CA). Almost certainly obtainable — **request the original from STAND and/or the partner**; STAND likely already holds these files.
> - **NEWS** = news-outlet copyright (WMAR/Scripps, FOX45/WBFF, CBS). **Do not reuse** without written permission; fine to link/embed for reference.
> - **ORG-SOCIAL** = the org's own social accounts (login-walled). **Request originals from STAND.**
> - **DO-NOT-USE** = sensitive (Angelo Little). Cataloged for awareness only.

## F. Recommendations (for the redesign)
1. **Fastest path to authentic, usable photos:** ask STAND directly for originals — they almost certainly hold the CCC blog photos (wellness circle, mentor-with-mentee, headshots), the Wilde Lake father-son flyer, and all social/merch assets. These are the on-mission, people-forward shots the new site needs.
2. **Coach Duncan specifically:** CCC headshot (A1) is the cleanest usable portrait. For "in action," the WMAR father-son photos/video (B1) and the CFHoCo grant photo (A2) are strong — request permission/originals.
3. **Credibility/impact imagery:** Howard County YES! Council + Youth Programming Update photos and Flickr album (A3) are usable **with credit** (confirm with the County) and reinforce the $100K funding / 96% attendance story.
4. **Verify before publishing:** Flickr per-photo license; County reuse policy; partner permissions in writing.
5. **Minors:** several authentic shots include youth participants — obtain photo-release confirmation from STAND before publishing any identifiable minor.
6. **Sensitive:** keep the Angelo Little material entirely out of marketing surfaces.
