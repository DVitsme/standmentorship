## Camping Trip 2023

S.T.A.N.D Mentorship outdoor enrichment trip, Maryland, 2023 — woods, creek/streambed, pond, an old stone railroad tunnel, and a campsite with dome tents. Activities seen: hiking wooded trails, fishing, water play at the creek, nature exploration, a mentor briefing, and a group cookout under a canopy. Subjects are teen boys (youth) and adult mentors; descriptions are kept general and respectful.
