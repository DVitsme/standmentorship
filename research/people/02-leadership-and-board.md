## Methodology & Confidence Key

- **CONFIRMED** = stated on STAND's own site **and/or** corroborated by an independent, authoritative third-party source (FDA.gov press listings, HCPSS staff directory, company website, major news outlet).
- **PARTIALLY CONFIRMED** = STAND site + a plausible but not definitive external match.
- **UNVERIFIED** = relies on STAND's bio only; no independent corroboration found, or the name is too common to disambiguate safely.
- Disambiguation risk is flagged per person. Several names (Michelle Jackson, James Wicks, Michael Franklin, Jahlil Jarrett) are common; matches were treated conservatively and **not** attributed unless strongly supported.

Primary STAND source for all bios below: **https://www.standmentorship.org/about** (the leadership/board bios live on the About page).

# LEADERSHIP TEAM

## 1. Dr. Tracey Wheatley — Chief Operations Officer
**STAND role: CONFIRMED** (listed as COO on standmentorship.org/about)

### STAND bio (verbatim/near-verbatim from site)
"An ordained Elder and servant leader who founded **Living Life Unbroken** ministry. She launched **Synergy Mobile Labs in 2023** to provide essential medical services. She shares her life with husband **Craig** and their **six adult children and four grandchildren**." (Matches the brief: ordained Elder, Living Life Unbroken, Synergy Mobile Labs 2023, married to Craig, six adult children.)

### Synergy Mobile Labs — CONFIRMED (fully characterized)
- **What it is:** A **mobile phlebotomy and diagnostic / allied-health services** company — it brings blood draws and health testing to clients in their homes/workplaces rather than requiring a clinic visit. Founded **2023** "to serve the needs of those who were unable to leave home on their own" and to "make blood draws convenient."
- **Website:** https://synergymobilelabs.com/ — **Instagram:** https://www.instagram.com/synergy_mobile_labs/
- **Location:** 580B Northern Ave, Hagerstown, MD 21742 (note: Hagerstown is in Western MD / Washington County, not Baltimore–Howard). Phone 240-500-9828; Info@SynergyMobileLabs.com; scheduling via meetings.hubspot.com/tracey-wheatley.
- **Services (from site):** mobile blood & specimen collection; DOT and non-DOT urine testing; EKG testing; alcohol & drug testing; fingerprinting & background checks; private-pay home draws for the uninsured; and **phlebotomy certification courses** via "Synergy Training Academy" (NPS certification).
- **So: NOT a STEM lab** — it is a medical/phlebotomy mobile lab plus a training academy. Wheatley is its **CEO/owner** (per LinkedIn).

### Living Life Unbroken — PARTIALLY CONFIRMED (it is a ministry, not the unrelated book)
- A **faith ministry** Wheatley founded; she is described (via her LinkedIn) as **"Servant of God, Motivational Speaker, and Leadership Instructor with Living Life Unbroken since 2011,"** and as its **Business Manager**, working as **Author, Teacher, Preacher**.
- **No standalone website or Facebook page was located** for "Living Life Unbroken" as an organization (searches returned unrelated MD churches). Treat its formal/online presence as **UNVERIFIED**.
- **Disambiguation flag:** Do **not** confuse with the book *Living Unbroken: Reclaiming Your Life and Your Heart After Divorce* by **Tracie Miles** (Proverbs 31 Ministries) — different person, different title, unrelated.

### Credentials & professional profile
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/tracey-wheatley-ph-d-cpt-cpi-ahi-445b9185/ — listed as **"Tracey Wheatley, Ph.D, CPT, CPI, AHI — Chief Executive Officer, Synergy Mobile Labs."** Based in **Baltimore, MD**.
- **Ph.D.** reported as **Theology / Biblical Studies, North Carolina School of Theology** (from search of her profile — treat as PARTIALLY CONFIRMED).
- Post-nominals (best interpretation; UNVERIFIED expansions): **CPT** = Certified Phlebotomy Technician; **CPI** = Certified Phlebotomy Instructor; **AHI** = Allied Health Instructor — consistent with running a phlebotomy training academy.

**Overall confidence: HIGH** on identity, COO role, Synergy Mobile Labs, and ministry existence. **MEDIUM** on the ministry's formal footprint and exact degree details.

## 4. Shelly L. Burgess — Board Chair
**STAND role: CONFIRMED** (listed as Board Chair on standmentorship.org/about) — strongest public record of the board.

### STAND bio
"**Washington D.C. native** with a **36-year career at FDA.** **Ordained Elder since 2003**, recognized for strategically guiding nonprofits toward sustainability. Holds **bachelor's in communications and master's in public affairs.**"

### FDA communications career — CONFIRMED
Burgess has an extensive, verifiable public record as an **FDA spokeswoman / press officer in the FDA Office of Media Affairs** — directly supporting the "Senior Advisor for Strategic Communications" characterization in the brief:
- Listed as an **FDA Office of Media Affairs press officer** — contact **shelly.burgess@fda.hhs.gov**, 301-796-4651 — appearing on FDA press releases (e.g., the FDA warning about **counterfeit Teva Adderall**). Source: FDA/LegiStorm press-release feed (https://www.legistorm.com/stormfeed/view_rss/225558/organization/31504/title/fda-warns-consumers-about-counterfeit-version-of-tevas-adderall.html).
- **Widely quoted in national media (2012–2013)** as the FDA spokeswoman on energy-drink and dietary-supplement safety:
  - **Monster Energy** deaths investigation (Oct 2012): "As with any reports of a death or injury the agency receives, we take them very seriously and investigate diligently." — CBS News (https://www.cbsnews.com/news/fda-investigates-five-deaths-one-heart-attack-linked-to-monster-energy-drinks/), Philadelphia Inquirer (https://www.inquirer.com/philly/health/20121023_FDA__MONSTER_CITED_AS_REAL__KILLER_BREW_.html), Fox News (https://www.foxnews.com/health/fda-says-5-deaths-heart-attack-reportedy-linked-to-monster-energy-drink).
  - Also referenced in coverage of **5-Hour Energy** and broader energy-drink/supplement reporting.
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/shelly-burgess-a62b454/ — "**Shelly Burgess — Senior Adviser at FDA**" (now retired).

### Discrepancies / disambiguation to flag
- **Hometown:** STAND's own About page says **"Washington D.C. native,"** whereas the project brief says **"from Columbia, MD."** Likely she *resides* in Columbia/Howard County (fits a Howard County board) but is *originally* from D.C. — **flagged conflict**, lean to "DC native, MD resident."
- **Master's degree:** STAND says **"master's in public affairs"**; brief says **"master's in communications."** — **flagged conflict**; STAND's own page says public affairs.
- **Tenure / "Air Force" claim:** Some aggregated search summaries asserted "**33 years of federal service, including 18 years in the U.S. Air Force, retired April 2025.**" This **could not be tied to a clean source** and **conflicts** with the "36-year FDA career" framing (one cannot have both 36 yrs at FDA and 18 yrs in the Air Force). **Treat the Air Force / exact-years detail as UNVERIFIED / possible conflation** with another "Shelly Burgess." The FDA communications spokeswoman identity itself is solid.
- **"Ordained Elder since 2003"** — stated on STAND; not independently verified, but consistent and plausible.

**Overall confidence: HIGH** that the STAND Board Chair is the FDA-spokeswoman Shelly Burgess (communications-career match is unambiguous). **MEDIUM** on biographical specifics (hometown, exact degree, exact years).
