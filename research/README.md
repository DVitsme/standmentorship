## Method & integrity notes
- Website captured via per-page fetches; history recovered via the Wayback Machine (`curl`, since web.archive.org is blocked to the fetch tool).
- External research ran as **6 parallel agents** (one — social media — hung on a login-wall fetch and was finished manually).
- **Confidence levels are flagged throughout**, CONFIRMED vs UNVERIFIED facts are separated, disambiguation of similarly-named orgs/people is documented, and **no figures were fabricated** (e.g., no financials are asserted beyond the public "<$50K, 990-N filer").
- A short list of **open questions** (mostly ~5-minute manual state-portal checks) is at the end of `SUMMARY.md`.

A 17-year-old S.T.A.N.D mentee was murdered at the Mall in Columbia in July 2024. This is real and central to the org's mission but must be handled with dignity and restraint in any public-facing work — never as a marketing device. See `SUMMARY.md` §7.
