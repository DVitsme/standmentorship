# Vision and Mission

**URL:** https://www.standmentorship.org/program-information
**Scraped:** 2026-06-17

## Vision Statement (verbatim)
> "Our vision is to be committed to strengthening, training, advancing, nurturing, and developing leaders who can continue a rippling effect of change for generations to come."

## Mission Statement (verbatim)
> "To empower individuals through transformative learning experiences and exceptional opportunities for educational and professional growth, ultimately enriching their lives."

## Decoding the acronym
The vision statement's five verbs map directly to **S.T.A.N.D**:
- **S**trengthening
- **T**raining
- **A**dvancing
- **N**urturing
- **D**eveloping

## Themes
- Leadership development with multi-generational ("rippling effect") impact.
- Educational + professional growth, transformative learning.
- Personal and professional trajectory enhancement.
