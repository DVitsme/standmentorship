Major Basis:

1. Mentoring(Community Service)
Provide positive role models for the young men to model themselves after. This will hopefully encourage these young men to be leaders and promote positive reinforcement.  We also require that these young men participate in community service.

2. Motivational Seminars/Engagements
Assist in boosting up the confidence and drive in these young men. Encouraging them to make a difference within themselves and within their community. The seminars will host different speakers that have advanced in spite of their circumstances and show the young men that even though their conditions may lead them to doubt their ability for success, that the right planning and right attitude may open doors and create opportunities for them.

3. Overall Health
During the transition from childhood to adulthood, adolescents establish patterns of behavior and make lifestyle choices that affect both their current and future health.  We want to educate them on making smart decisions concerning their over health.

4. Academics/Tutoring
Education is number one.  Through the tutoring offering during this time, we expect to see gradual results in the young men over the school year

5. Crisis/Peer Support
A crisis is not necessarily a traumatic situation or event, but to a person’s reaction to an event. Like puberty for example, we do not want to provide psychotherapy, but instead to offer short-term intervention to help each young man receive the support and the necessary resources needed.

6. Talent Development
Each child has untapped potential in them. Our goal is to help them recognize their inner gifts  and put it use within their community.

7. Sport Training

We have partnered with the Eastside Colts Football team providing each child with the   opportunity to participate in physical activities.
