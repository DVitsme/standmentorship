Program Philosophy:

Our program is committed to providing the best care possible for each participant. We use a cognitive based curriculum that is free of stereotypes. It is designed to meet the need of each child through various classes that focus on the physical, emotional, intellectual and social skills. We believe that the interactions experienced in these classes will help in the child's growth and development.

Program Objectives:

The primary program objective is to provide a safe and warm atmosphere for the child during the programs operating period. Each child is encouraged to participate in all activities during this time.
We expect each child to:
(1) develop and maintain a positive feeling about themselves and their ability to learn and create.
(2) expand their awareness and experiences outside of their community
(3) understand the high and lows of life. Know how to deal with success and failure.
(4) develop the ability to interact with individuals of various cultures, backgrounds, gender, ages, race, etc.

Program Operation:

The program will operate Monday through Friday from 2:45p.m.-4:45pm after school.
Inclement Weather:
When Baltimore City Schools are closed due to inclement weather, STAND will not be in operation. Also, this includes any time that Afterschool activities are canceled by the City. Impending weather may also cause us to cancel for that particular day.

Program Staffing:

We have adequate amount of staffing that allows us to provide the attention needed to each group of kids. Some of our staff include school teachers, trained mentors, volunteers and coaches. All staff has completed an extensive criminal background check in order to be considered for employment. Some of our staff is also trained in CPR and First Aid.

Communication:

The After School Program maintains an open door policy. Please feel free to direct questions or concerns related to any facet of the After School Program to the Program Directors at any time. Any questions regarding policies, staffing, activities, or a child’s participation in the Program may be directed to the Program Director.

Medication Policy

The After School Program will not dispense prescription and non-prescription medication.

Pick-up Procedures

We  utilizes a checkout system to ensure that each child is dismissed to the proper person at the end of the day. Children will not be allowed to leave the After School Program unattended. Parents must walk into the school building and checkout with the Afterschool Staff member. The children can help with this responsibility by getting into the habit of saying goodbye to their teacher. Children will not be released to anyone other than a parent, guardian, or person listed on the child’s permission form unless the child’s parent notifies the After School Program prior to pickup time. If a staff member is unfamiliar with any person picking up a child, the staff member will ask to see a picture identification before the child is released. We are only responsible for your child when he/she attends the program.  Their prompt arrival to the program is expected.  It is not our responsibility to make sure your child attends daily.

HALF-DAYS

The After School Program is closed.
